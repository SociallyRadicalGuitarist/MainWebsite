Welcome

Get ready to learn about the versatility of the guitar and get ready to swallow that red pill. The world is not as it seems. That will be the lesson and purpose of this show. If you had a sneaking suspicion that contemporary politics don't work, you're right. This show hopes to offer solutions, all while warming your earbuds with beautiful music from Canada and around the world. This Show airs every Saturday from 3pm to 4pm on CKMS 102.7.
